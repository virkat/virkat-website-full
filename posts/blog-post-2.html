The Journey: From CS to Data

This section explores my motivation for starting this path. My Computer Science studies taught me the 'how' of technology, but I wanted to understand the 'why' hidden in data. The Google Data Analytics Certificate was the perfect bridge between these two worlds.

Accessible to All

The program is designed for beginners with no prior experience required, making it a perfect entry point into the world of data.

Flexible & Affordable

At just $49/month via Coursera, and completable in 3-6 months (approx. 240 hours), it fit perfectly alongside my university studies.

Structured Learning

The certificate provides a clear, structured path from foundational concepts to practical, job-ready skills.

The Toolkit: Skills for Success

This section details the core technologies and skills I acquired. The certificate provides a comprehensive toolkit for any aspiring data analyst. Click on each tool to learn more about how it was covered in the program.

SQL: The Language of Data

I learned that SQL is the main language for working with data. The certificate taught me how to query huge datasets, find specific information, and combine data from different sources. This skill is crucial for getting the most up-to-date information for any analysis.

R & Tableau: Analysis and Visualization

R is a powerful programming language for statistical analysis and creating charts. I learned to use it to clean data and perform calculations. When it was time to present my findings, Tableau's easy, drag-and-drop interface helped me create interactive dashboards and turn my data into a visual story anyone could understand.

AI as a Productivity Tool

The course focused on how AI can help with daily tasks. I learned that AI can assist with tedious jobs like cleaning data, writing complex formulas, and even brainstorming ideas for charts. It's like having a smart assistant that makes you more efficient and productive.

The Story: Beyond the Numbers
